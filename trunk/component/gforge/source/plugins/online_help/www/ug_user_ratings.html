3.4. User ratings

You can be rated by other users and you can rate other users. Every time you go to the detail of a user, you can rate the user.

Ratings can be given for:

  • Teamwork/Attitude

  • Coding

  • Design/Architecture

  • Follow-Trough/Reliability

  • Leadership/Management
