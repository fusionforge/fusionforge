Contacting Us

Please send any comments, questions, or suggestions to the ViewVC users mailing list. There is also a mailing list specifically for ViewVC developers. You can subscribe to these lists, as well view the list archives, (and other project lists) here.

ViewVC is an Open Source project, and all contributions are welcome.
